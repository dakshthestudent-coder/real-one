How I Work

Simple steps,
no confusion.

Getting a website made with me is very easy. You don't need to know anything about tech — I take care of all that. Here is what happens step by step.

01
We Talk

You tell me what you need — I listen. We decide together how the website should look and when it should be ready. Simple conversation, no forms.

02
I Make a Design

I show you how the website will look before building it. You can tell me what you like or don't like — we keep changing till it feels right.

03
I Build It

I make the website and show you the progress at every step. No surprises — you can see what is happening all the time.

04
It Goes Live

Your website goes on the internet. I check it on mobile, laptop, and everything — so it works properly for every person who visits.
